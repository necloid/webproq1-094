— Local Food

Padang is famous for its bold and spicy cuisine with a variety of dishes served to share. Some iconic favorites include rendang (spicy slow-cooked beef), sate padang (skewered meat with thick savory sauce), gulai ayam (chicken curry), ayam pop (lightly fried chicken), and many more.

My personal top picks are definitely rendang and sate padang!
