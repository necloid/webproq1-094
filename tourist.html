— Tourist Places

Padang has plenty of beautiful spots to visit, from stunning beaches like Pantai Air Manis and Pantai Padang, to iconic landmarks like Jam Gadang in nearby Bukittinggi. You can also explore Lembah Anai Waterfall or take a boat trip to Mentawai Islands, famous for surfing and breathtaking views.
